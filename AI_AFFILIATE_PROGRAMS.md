## Key Takeaways

1. **Best commission rates:** Canva (30%), Jasper (30%), ElevenLabs (30%), Writesonic (30%)
2. **Longest cookies:** Canva & Grammarly (90 days), Writesonic (90 days)
3. **Easiest to join:** Most programs have no minimum traffic or approval requirements
4. **No affiliate programs:** OpenAI (ChatGPT) and Anthropic (Claude) do not have public affiliate programs
5. **Credit-based instead of cash:** Midjourney, Codeium/Windsurf offer platform credits rather than monetary commissions
6. **Enterprise-focused:** GitHub Copilot requires Microsoft affiliate approval; OpenAI/Anthropic partnerships are B2B-oriented
